Chartbeat Illustrations

tldr; Made a bunch of product illustrations for Chartbeat’s support site and marketing materials.

tools:: Sketch, Adobe Illustrator.

I like drawing. I did it a lot as a kid, and now as a 20-something kid I'm doing it with computers. It started because I needed graphics and illustrations for the Chartbeat projects I was working on and didn't really have the weight to get any of the actual designers' time. Also I don't really know much about image copyrights and am deathly afraid of breaking the law.

So I decided to just start making them myself—the three graphics for the Support Site homepage were the first ones that I made using Sketch.

As soon as I was able to make those, I realized how many more illustrations we needed for the other parts of the site, and got to work on a slew of others to use as timeless images of Chartbeat's products.

One of these things is not like the other...

These started out just as the cute little illustrations I did for our Support Site project, but they quickly took on a life of their own and even found their way into some of Chartbeat’s actual Marketing collateral.

Check them out on the first two pages of the Chartbeat Publishing Welcome Package!
